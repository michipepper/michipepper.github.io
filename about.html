About me

Product Designer & Manager with 3+ years of experience, passionate about AI-native products, startups, and design that creates real human impact. Currently Product Designer at TeamSystem, building an AI-native product in the HR tech field from 0 to 1, while trying to build something that’s genuinely useful to people in the long-term care sector.

I have a strong systems-thinking mindset and a genuine love for building products grounded in research, aligned with business goals, and designed with intention. Previously, I gained agency and client-facing experience during my university years, learning early how to translate business needs into thoughtful design.

Outside of work, you’ll find me at the cinema, inside a museum, planning a new trip or writing my newsletter from a good café. Come check it out :) michipepper.substack.com
