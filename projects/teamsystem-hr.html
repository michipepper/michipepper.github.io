Exploring AI inside an enterprise HR suite

Targeted product evolution and AI experimentation across an enterprise platform for people management.

Role Product Designer
Industry B2B HR Suite
Scope Strategic Design & AI Exploration
Company TeamSystem

TeamSystem HR is a cloud-based suite for people management, HR processes, and workforce administration — serving mid-to-large companies that have outgrown simpler tools.

What I work on

I contribute to targeted product-evolution initiatives, leading exploratory discovery and experimenting with how AI can enhance product features and user experience. This happens in close collaboration with the Product Owner and other product designers on the team.

Recent focus areas have included OrgChart, Timesheet, and the People App — each with its own discovery loop, prototyping cycle, and AI exploration.

How AI shows up

The interesting questions in a product like this aren’t “can we add a chatbot.” They’re: where does AI actually save time for an HR manager? Where does it make a decision better? Where does it remove a step instead of adding one? My contribution here is in the upstream exploration — running discovery sessions, prototyping interactions, and giving the team something concrete to react to.
