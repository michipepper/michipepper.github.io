Nicer — discount codes, right when you need them

A B2C mobile app designed from concept to MVP. Solving a small but real frustration: finding the right promo code at the right time.

Role Product Designer
Industry B2C Mobile App
Scope Concept → MVP
Year 2021 — 2022

Many people remember that an influencer once shared a useful promo code — but can’t find it when they actually need it. Nicer simplifies that experience by storing and organizing promo codes based on the user’s personal interests, making online shopping smoother and more cost-effective.

I worked on Nicer from initial concept through to MVP, covering product strategy, UX, UI, and visual identity.
