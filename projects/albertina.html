Albertina Academy — a public institution online

End-to-end redesign of the official website of Accademia Albertina di Belle Arti, in full compliance with Designers Italia’s guidelines for public administration.

Role Product Designer & Researcher
Industry Public Administration · Art Institution
Scope Discovery to Delivery
Year 2022 — 2023

The website of Accademia Albertina di Belle Arti was reimagined and designed from the ground up, in full compliance with the guidelines for public administration websites issued by Designers Italia — while taking into account the needs of all users involved.

I led user research, competitor analysis, interviews, customer journey mapping, flowcharts, wireframes, and accessible UI design. The project ran from discovery to delivery and was selected after an internal search for the team to lead the redesign.

The outcome is a website that reflects the essence of the institution while respecting the accessibility and clarity standards expected from public services.
