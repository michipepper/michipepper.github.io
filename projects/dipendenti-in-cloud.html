Owning features in an HR cloud platform

Leading the design of Dipendenti in Cloud — the HR software business owners, admins, and HR teams use to manage people every day.

Role Product Designer & Owner
Industry B2B2C HR SaaS
Scope Feature Ownership
Company TeamSystem

Dipendenti in Cloud is the simplest HR management software on the Italian market — designed for small businesses that need to manage attendance, leave, expenses, and people without learning a new language.

I lead the design of the cloud software and its companion app for business owners, administrators, and HR teams. Together with the Product Manager, we translate business and user needs into strategic product decisions, and I own specific features end-to-end.

What I’ve owned

  • Dashboard redesign — rethinking the entry point of the product around what users actually need to see at a glance, across web and mobile.
  • Automatic renewal flow — designing a subscription renewal experience that’s clear, fair, and reduces churn without dark patterns.
  • Pricing and licenses — the surface where business model meets product. Hard to get right, easy to get wrong.

How I work in this product

Feature ownership in a fast-moving SaaS means moving between many things: leading discovery with UX researchers, translating insights into product decisions, defining specs and acceptance criteria with PO and developers, aligning with business goals, contributing to roadmap prioritization, and shipping end-to-end with engineering and analytics.

It also means accountability for outcomes. Not just for designs that look good in Figma.
