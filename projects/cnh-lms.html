Redesigning an LMS for a construction giant

An end-to-end redesign of CNH-Case’s Web Academy — the corporate training platform used by dealers worldwide.

Role Product Designer
Industry Enterprise LMS
Scope End-to-end redesign
Year 2023

CNH-Case’s Web Academy is the corporate training platform for their global dealer network. The redesign covered the entire product lifecycle — processes, training methods, and user experience — with the goal of meeting end-user needs more effectively and making learning more intuitive and engaging.

The project was end-to-end: from discovery and user research with dealers, through information architecture and interaction design, to high-fidelity UI design and hand-off.
